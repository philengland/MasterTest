Sticky Footers

A "Sticky" Footer is a Footer element that is stuck to the bottom of the contents with various behavioral options, depending on the desired effect.

 

This website goes into more complete depth about the various options for Sticky Footers and covers general cases for how to implement them, which can be adapted to the <div> structure used by Flare when Building HTML5 output.

 

One of the most common cases is to create a Footer that will always be at the bottom of the content in the Topic, but will "magnetize" to the lower extremity of the body-container <div> even when a short Topic's contents will not reach that low to "push" it down there.

Features:

  • Footer will stay at the bottom of contents, whether contents are short or long
  • Footer will "stick" to the bottom of the screen, even when contents are short
  • Footer will not be shown until scrolled to for longer contents
